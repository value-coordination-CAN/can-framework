CAN is an open research platform for studying post-monetary coordination, digital governance, AI accountability, and socio-technical resilience.

## Research opportunities
- pilot evaluation studies
- behavioural experiments
- algorithm auditing (bias, explainability)
- governance design and institutional legitimacy
- longitudinal impact studies

## Ethics and data
Human-subject research requires consent and ethics approval (IRB or equivalent). Use privacy-preserving analytics and publish methods openly where possible.

## How to engage
- submit a research proposal via GitHub Issues
- contribute evaluation metrics and benchmark datasets
- run living labs and publish results (including null results)
